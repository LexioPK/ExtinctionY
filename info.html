Pokemon Extinction Y (V 1.0)(

The Game

Welcome to Pokémon Extinction Y. This is a rom hack of Pokémon Y that tries to make the game both chanllenging and fun. Rather than the exterme hardcore hacks, this one aims to give the player a ton of tools to help them get throught the game. Hopefully that doesn't make the game too difficult while still maintaing a good level of challenge. In this Hack you will find:

  • Drastic changes for every Pokémon in the from of Base Stats, Types, Abilities, Evolutions, and/or Move Pools
  • Almost every Pokémon is avaliable throughout the main story (No Box Legendaries till post game)
  • Over 300 overhauled trainer battles with an emphasis on Single & Double Battles with a few others mixed in
  • A variety of useful items spread throught the region
  • Updated Game Mechanics: Gen 7 onward Burn (1/16th dmg) & a Tweaked Type chart
  • Various quaility of life changes such as: Rare Candies, Removed EV's, Buffed Catch Rates & Standardized EXP

How to Patch

Step-by-step instructions for playing the Rom Hack.

  • Find yourself an 3ds Emulator
  • Find yourself a blank rom of Pokémon Y
  • Put everthing in "Mods" in to your Emulators mods folder
  • Not sure if this works for both version of Y but it might
